Red Raider Loyalty

Athletic Fee Required

To reserve spots and earn points, you need to have an active athletic fee on file with Texas Tech University. The fee gives you access to all home athletic events.

What you get
Access to reserve spots for Football & Basketball
Points for every event you attend
Entry to lower-demand sports (Soccer, Volleyball, Baseball & more)
Waitlist access at high-demand games if you don't have a claim
Purchase Athletic Fee — TTU Student Business

Already paid? It can take up to 24 hours to appear in the system. Log out and back in with your TTU eRaider credentials to refresh your status.
